Elevator Falls Five Floors with Student Trapped Inside in NYC

An elevator in Brookdale Residence Hall of Yeshiva University in NYC fell five floors with a female Brookdale resident trapped inside on April 11, the student newspaper The Commentator reports. The student was stuck inside the elevator for about 45 min as the elevator fell from the fifth floor to the basement, then rose to the first floor and fell again, becoming stuck between the basement and first floor.

TKE Acquires Albany Elevator in New York’s Capital Region

TKE has acquired Albany Elevator, a leading provider of modernization, maintenance, repair and installation services in the Albany, New York Capital Region for the past 21 years, the company announced on May 3. Terms of the deal were not disclosed, but Lift Business Advisors facilitated the transaction on behalf of Albany Elevator.

AEG Introduces Tuition Reimbursement Program

American Elevator Group (AEG), headquartered in NYC, has introduced a tuition reimbursement program for its approximately 650 employees nationwide not covered by a collective bargaining agreement. The initiative aims to deliver the same benefits as those available to employees covered by union-regulated programs.

92 Otis Escalators Part of Munich Metro Modernization

Through 2026, Otis Germany will install 92 heavy-duty NPETM escalators at 18 stations of the Munich Metro, bringing the number of escalators the OEM has supplied to the system since 1985 to 500. Part of an ongoing modernization, the escalators will be manufactured at Otis’ facility in Breclav, Czech Republic.
